Weather Forecast: A weak trough will move into Western NY early this afternoon, and may produce a few scattered showers. Otherwise, mainly dry and very warm weather will continue today through tonight. Strong low pressure will pass just west of the region Sunday, bringing gusty winds, periods of rain, and possibly a few thunderstorms to the region. The combination of rain and rapid snowmelt may bring flooding to some areas east of Lake Ontario Sunday through next week. Mainly dry weather will return Monday and last through Wednesday, with one cool day Monday giving way to another strong warming trend through the middle of next week.
