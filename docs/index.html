Weather Forecast: High pressure over the Upper Great Lakes this morning will drift by just to our north this afternoon. This will enable most areas to experience partly to mostly sunny weather with notably less wind than that from Thursday. Several waves of low pressure will then move out of the Ohio valley during the weekend to support a soaking rain. The bulk of this will fall late tonight into early Sunday with many areas picking up at least an inch to an inch and a half of rainfall.
