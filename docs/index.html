Weather Forecast: High pressure will drift off the Mid-Atlantic coast this evening into tonight. Meanwhile, a passing weak cold front will bring a brief round of rain and snow showers to areas east of Lake Ontario this evening, with a few widely scattered rain showers across the Finger Lakes and western Southern Tier. Elongated low pressure will then extend eastward across our area later Friday through the weekend, bringing more widespread unsettled weather that will feature periods of rain for most areas, and possibly some mixed precipitation across the North Country. Expect a large north to south spread in temperatures over the weekend owed to a wavy frontal boundary draped across the area. This will be maximized on Saturday with daytime highs near freezing toward the Saint Lawrence Valley, and upper 60s to near 70 across the western Southern Tier.
