While this feature will generate a soaking rain across our region...there will be several hours of near rainfree weather as well. Rainfall through tonight will range from a half inch in most areas...to an inch or so across the western Southern Tier. Leftover rain and wet snow showers late tonight will then be pushed south of the area on Sunday...as high pressure will briefly build in for the end of the weekend.
