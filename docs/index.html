Weather Forecast: In the wake of a cold front a notably cooler airmass will overspread our region tonight and Wednesday...when high temperatures will be confined to the 30s and 40s. The same frontal boundary will then serve as a pathway for a wave of low pressure that could spread some light mixed rain or wet snow over parts of the area Wednesday afternoon and evening. After this brief interruption...temperatures will return to early Spring levels with increasing day to day warmth into the weekend. Unfortunately...we can also count on a fair amount of rain this weekend...especially on Sunday.
