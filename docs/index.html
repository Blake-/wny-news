Weather Forecast: Lake snows southeast of the lakes will push north and weaken tonight...but not before dropping a few more inches of snow on areas including Buffalo and Watertown. A series of frontal boundaries will then push through our region Thursday and Thursday night...and this will bring a general nuisance snowfall of an inch or two to the region. Areas east of the lakes could see upwards of four inches. After a break on Friday...and very strong arctic cold front will plow through the area on Saturday with mixed rain and snow. The coldest airmass in at least a couple years will follow for late Sunday through the first half of next week.
