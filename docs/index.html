Weather Forecast: West to northwest flow of seasonably cold air will continue to support accumulating lake effect snow southeast of the lakes through the rest of the weekend. Additional accumulations off Lake Erie will be modest and focused across the higher terrain inland from the lake. The snow will be much heavier southeast of Lake Ontario, especially across northern Cayuga and Oswego counties later today through Sunday morning with very hazardous travel expected. Lake effect snow will gradually dwindle later Sunday through Sunday night, although a few scattered light snow showers will linger south of the lakes through early next week.
