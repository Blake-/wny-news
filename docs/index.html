Weather Forecast: Rain will change to wet snow from west to east this evening, with minor accumulations across higher terrain. Heavy lake effect snow will then develop east of Lake Erie and Lake Ontario tonight, with bands of heavy snow continuing through Thursday night off Lake Erie and through Friday evening off Lake Ontario. Strong winds and very heavy snowfall will create near whiteout conditions in lake effect areas and extremely hazardous travel. Meanwhile, outside of lake effect snow, it will be mostly sunny Thursday through Friday. The cold and lake effect will only last two days, with a notable warming trend next weekend.
