Weather Forecast: Weakening low pressure will pass by to the south of the region Friday through Friday night, generating widespread light snowfall. Accumulations will be modest, but will be enough to produce slippery travel. A very cold airmass will then pour into the region behind this system over the weekend, with occasional light lake effect snow showers southeast of Lake Ontario and Lake Erie with additional light accumulations. High pressure will bring a return to dry weather by Sunday evening, with cold giving way to more seasonable temperatures early next week.
