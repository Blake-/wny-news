Weather Forecast: A trough of low pressure will cross the eastern Great Lakes this afternoon and evening, with widespread light snow and minor accumulations. Lake enhancement will add several additional inches of accumulation east of Lake Erie and Lake Ontario this evening through Friday morning. Dry weather will briefly return Friday afternoon through Friday night, along with a short warming trend. A strong cold front will then cross the region Saturday, with a mix of rain and snow changing to all snow as colder air moves back into the area. A frigid arctic airmass will then build into the region Sunday night through the middle of next week, with dangerously cold wind chills areawide, and accumulating lake effect snow east of the lakes.
