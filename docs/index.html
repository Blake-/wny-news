Weather Forecast: Surface high pressure centered over the central Great Lakes this afternoon will cross New York State through tonight into the first half of Tuesday. A weak warm front will fall apart as it works its way northeast towards the region Tuesday, supporting a chance for a few rain showers across the far western Southern Tier Tuesday afternoon. Conditions will then deteriorate late Wednesday through the end of the week as a large complex storm system will gradually progress across the Great Lakes to New England. With this system, expect rain and snow, with the first accumulating snow of the season for many areas, especially across the higher terrain.
