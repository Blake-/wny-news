Weather Forecast: An area of low pressure passing by to our south will bring a mix of rain and wet snow this afternoon and evening, before transitioning to some lake effect snow showers east of the lakes tonight. High pressure will then bring an end to any lingering lake induced snow showers Thursday. High pressure maintains mainly dry weather Thursday night before a weak clipper system brings a general light snowfall to the area to end the work week. An arctic blast in the wake of this system will then deliver the coldest air of this young winter season this weekend, along with some light lake effect snow showers south of the lakes.
